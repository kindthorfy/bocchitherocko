About Bocchi The Rock

Bocchi the Rock! is a band comedy 4-koma manga series written and illustrated by Aki Hamazi. The manga series serialized in Manga Time Kirara MAX by Houbunsha since the February 2018 issue and collected five volumes in tankōbon format as of November 2022. It became a popular series for its successor of K-ON! and ranked 8th place in the Next Manga Awards 2019.

An anime series produced by CloverWorks premiered from October 9 to December 25, 2022, with two theater compilations—the first part screened on June 7, 2024, and the second part on August 9, 2024.

In this story, Hitori Gotoh is a high school girl who's starting to learn to play the guitar because she dreams of being in a band. However, she's so shy that she hasn't made a single friend. Her dream might come true after she meets Nijika Ijichi, a girl who plays drums and is looking for a new guitarist for her band.
